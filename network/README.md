# Frugal AI Network

This section will hold the Frugal AI Network: the community of Commonwealth institutions, ministries, and practitioners building, localising, and operating Frugal AI. It gives the growing alliance behind the [Gaborone to New Delhi Compact](https://www.col.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Gaborone-to-New-Delhi-Compact.pdf) a working home — a place to share builds, localisations, measurements, and lessons rather than start from zero.

It is under development. Until it fills in, participation runs through COL's [Frugal AI programme](https://www.col.org/frugal).

## Planned contents

- A showcase of member builds and localisations, from the first chat service to full stacks.
- A localisation exchange: curriculum alignments, language coverage notes, and worked examples by country.
- A contribution guide for sharing components, measurements, and skills back to the network.
- Contact points for joining the network.
